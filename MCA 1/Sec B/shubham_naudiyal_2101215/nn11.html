INTRODUCTION:

The duration of MCA course is total two academic years. It is a full-time postgraduate course offered by many colleges/Universities situated in India. This course has been divided into four semesters with the duration of six months each. The first year of this course focuses on the skill developments in computers, second year is aimed for providing a conceptual framework and the third year offers the specializations and project work. Eligibility: Students who have done their graduation in any discipline like Science, Commerce, Arts, Engineering, BCA from any recognized university are eligible to apply for this course. Aspirants should have studied Mathematics subject in the 10+2 standard or graduation level. The qualifying examination must be passed with 50% marks (45% for SC/ST category candidates).
